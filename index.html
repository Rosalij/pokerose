our story

Poké Rose was created with a simple idea: serve high-quality poke with care and attention to detail. Drawing from Hawaiian tradition and Japanese technique, we focus on clean flavors, fresh ingredients, and well-balanced combinations.

Our menu is built around responsibly sourced seafood, seasonal produce, and house-made dressings — nothing overdone, nothing unnecessary. Just honest food, made well. We’re here to offer a quieter, more thoughtful take on poke.
